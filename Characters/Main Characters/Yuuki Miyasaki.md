Name: Yuuki Miyasaki
A boy who is the embodiment of kind justice.
Age: 18 years old
Occupation: Cadet of the NSF.
Relatives:
Unnamed Father/Mother
Little Sister in elementary school.

Favourite Series: the cartoon Starlight Knights

Appearance: Short blue hair, and blue eyes. Many people have described as a very handsome man who presence eases people. His artificat is a black longsword. His battle attire which consists of a black hooded jacket, long black pants and grey strapped boots and black gloves.

Personality: He is very kind by nature and has been described by others as having very gentle eyes and a compassionate persona. He exhibits a great deal of determination and will not give up once he has a goal to achieve. He is very protective of his friends. He has difficulty lying, and doing so causes his face to contort severely as his honest nature clashes with the insincerity of his words. He has a distorted sense of values where he only finds self-worth from helping people without any compensation, feeling that the very act "helping people" is its own reward. He believes it is highly unfair that some people survive and others do not. When it comes down to receiving an injury or even giving up his life to help someone, he will do it without a second thought. The people who see this side of him are often very worried and attempt to correct his behavior, though they are unable to change his opinions. In the face of irrational evil, he shows a more cold and terrifying side of himself, acting more violently towards his opponents as a result.
