Appearance: Beautiful long white hair, and blue eyes. She is the light and the voice of reason in the Kavallier. Gentle, graceful and kind, she embodies the gentleness of a saintess along with the fierceness of a valkyrie. She is a doting mother and a caring lover, but can be strict with Noah whenever necessary. She is also sweet and innocent, trying to see the good in people and never giving up hope when it comes to fighting for the better, and cautious whenever she gets into a fight. In the past, Maria used to have a sad personality despite fighting the Neurovores, until she met Noah Kavallier and started becoming more cheerful with him.

#### CAS ability: 
**Time Manipulation**: She is able to alter the flow of time of the world to his will but she can't use more than one technique at a time. His powers make white energy vines appear during activation and also when time takes course. Her powers are inferior compared to Kevin, but her experience outdoes his by a thousand.

- **_Heed me, all creation, and fall still_:** Time is stopped for everyone except specific targets of her choice, but it takes a second to cast it, leaving her open to attacks before she can stop time. He's able to hold time up to 10 minutes, and after a short break again for another 10 minutes.

- **_Heed me, time, and return_:** a localized target's physical state is returned to a shortly before state. The target can even be himself, but it takes up to around 3 seconds to reverse the body's state relatively to how much it's damaged.

- **Time Acceleration:** it can be used to accelerate a specific target's natural healing so even the deadliest injuries can be treated as long the subject is alive.
