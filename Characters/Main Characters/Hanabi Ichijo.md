Hanabi Ichijo
Hanabi has purple eyes and auburn red hair tied back with a red hairband. She opts for the Makato variant of the Blk Battle Attire which consists of a long jacket with one long sleeve, a short skirt with a small pair of shorts, a pair of gloves and a utility belt.
A girl who is always bright and energetic. She was Kevin's childhood friend and is a hard worker who does everything to the best of her ability. She hates injustice and tends to act before thinking, rushing headlong into things. On the other hand, she is extremely kind at her core, and tends to suppress her true feelings as not to trouble those around her. She has a heavy crush on Kevin, that she hides to the best of her ability.
CAS Ability: Pyrokinesis
Personal Weapon: Torch
