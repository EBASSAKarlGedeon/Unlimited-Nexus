Name: Noah Kavallier
One of the Famous Commandant of the NSF. Descendant of the legendary Void Hunter, Karson Kavallier.
Age: 38 years old
Occupation: Void Hunter
Achievements: 
- 5400 registered Neurovores eliminated.
- His Platoon has a near perfect mission success rate.
Military Rank: Septentrion Seventh Class Captain
Relatives: 
Maria Mitchell (Wife)
Kevin Kavallier (Son)
Kiana Kavallier (Daughter)

Appearance: Typical of a male Noah, Noah is a large, tall, and muscled man, with a square jaw and messy, long white hair held in a ponytail and cerulean eyes. Ever since he became a NSF officer, Noah dons a heavy duty, inner-yellow and navy tailcoat with a wooly neckrest, armored shoulder pads and fingerless brown gloves. Under the coat, he wears a murky green, partially zipped up vest and brown trousers with knee high black boots. "Snowy" is typically a very easy-going guy, but with the years of hardships and too many goodbyes, he became more determined to protect those he cherished. "Snowy" has always been a very hard-headed person, who, with his mind and heart set to something, it would be difficult to shake his resolve or prevent him from getting back on his feet. He is relentless in his pursuit of his view on the Kavallier Oath, which slightly differs as he holds himself accountable for those he loved before the general public, and believed in protecting these beautiful sides of the world. 
"Snowy" favourite slogan : "Fight for all that is beautiful in the world".

#### CAS ability: 
**Sclerokinesis**: Makes the player completely immune to damage for a short period of time. Any hits taken will lower the amount of time active. The difference is that Noah can temporarily harden the user's skin to an impenetrable state. It provides complete immunity to damage, status effects, and knockbacks for a short duration, making it ideal for withstanding heavy attacks or maintaining attack momentum. He could even withstand a nuke in this state or The divine key heat.
### Personal Artifact: Noah Kavallier's primary weapon is the Judgment of Shamash, the 7th Divine Key, which he typically wields in its twin pistol or combines into a massive flaming greatsword (Zweihander). **Judgment of Shamash:** A Divine Key with immense fire-destruction capabilities that can incinerate users. Noah can survive by hardening his body to its limit.
