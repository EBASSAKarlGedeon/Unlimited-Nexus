Name: Seto Narukami
CAS Ability: Electrokinesis
Rank: Captain
His Platoon is comprised of:
- Yuuki Miyasaki
- Naomi Randall
- Nagi Karman
- Hanabi Ichijo
- Kevin Kavallier
Appearance: Blond hair, and blue eyes
Weapon: Kodachi
The Septentrion Seventh Class who works as an OSF platoon commander. He is a cheerful, eloquent young man who is humble despite his high ability and will lend a hand to anyone in trouble with nonchalant concern. He likes where he works and has refused all promotions, so his rank isn't much to brag about, but he attracts people to him with his charming character.
Due to the nature of service in the OSF, people die in battle. However, Seto's strong sense of responsibility means he does everything in his power to keep those serving under him alive. It seems this behavior is the product of his inherent kindness and some hard experiences he's had in the past.
