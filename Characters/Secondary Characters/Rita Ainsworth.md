## Rita Ainsworth

**Affiliation:** Victoria Housekeeping Co.  
**Role:** Senior Maid / Enforcer (second only to Sebastian)

**Appearance:**  
Rita Ainsworth possesses honey-blonde hair cut to neck length at the back, with longer strands framing her face. Her reddish-pink eyes and ever-present gentle smile give her a sweet, almost disarming aura. Her left eye remains covered, yet her perception is unaffected. She wears an elegant maid outfit with pink accents, adorned with roses—on her frilled headband, lace gloves, and even a delicate anklet. Dark leggings and heels complete her refined yet ominous silhouette.
Weapon: Scythe

**Personality:**  
Rita embodies duality. Outwardly, she is graceful, polite, and meticulous—performing every task with perfection. Beneath that lies a cold, highly efficient enforcer. She treats combat like choreography, eliminating targets with precision and calm detachment. Despite this, she remains teasing, mysterious, and surprisingly attentive, showing genuine care through small, thoughtful gestures.

### CAS Ability: _Murderer_

Rita can erase her presence from perception and environment.

- **Existence Concealment:** She suppresses all detectable signals—visual, thermal, cognitive—making her effectively “absent” from reality.
- **Silent Motion:** All sound generated by her actions is nullified, allowing perfect stealth.

Combined, she becomes an undetectable entity, striking without warning as if she was never there.
