## Organization

Known publicly as the **Kuroshōkai**, the group is formally registered under the name **Aeternum Economic Alliance (AEA)**, a powerful consortium of Japan’s largest corporations, research institutions, and financial entities. Operating at the intersection of economy, technology, and CAS development, the AEA exerts significant influence over global markets and strategic innovation.

Behind its corporate structure lies a unified objective: controlling the flow of resources, knowledge, and emerging technologies to shape future world order. Its members fund major projects, including Artifact production, neural research, and defense contracts tied to the NSF.

At its helm stands **Otto Apocalypse**, the enigmatic Head of Alliance, whose long-term vision and calculated decisions have positioned the AEA as one of the most dominant forces in the modern era.
