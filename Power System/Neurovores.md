## The Neurovores

Neurovores are non-human biological entities that appeared worldwide shortly after the first recorded CAS users. Their defining behavior is the consumption of **human neural tissue**, particularly the brain.

Research indicates that Neurovores do not simply feed for nutrition. Instead, they absorb **cognitive patterns stored in neural structures**. By consuming brains, they acquire fragments of knowledge, allowing them to expand their own CAS capabilities.

This makes them uniquely dangerous: the more intelligent their victims, the more rapidly they evolve.

Early encounters revealed that Neurovores specifically target:

- scientists
    
- engineers
    
- CAS users
    
- individuals with high cognitive density
    

Because of this behavior, many researchers believe Neurovores are driven by an instinct to accumulate **Omniscience**.
