# Cognitive Domain — Absolute Actuation Field (AAF)

A **Cognitive Domain** is the highest expression of CAS ability, where an Actuant temporarily constructs a **localized reality layer** governed by their own cognitive framework.

Within this zone, the user approaches **temporary perfect synchronization between Omniscience and Omnipotence**.

---

## Core Effects

### 1. **Total Amplification**

All CAS abilities used inside the domain are drastically enhanced.

- near-zero execution delay
- maximum output efficiency
- minimal cognitive loss

---

### 2. **Cognitive Supremacy**

The user gains **temporary hyper-omniscience** within the domain.

- perfect environmental awareness
- predictive modeling of opponents
- instant adaptation

---

### 3. **Absolute Actuation Priority**

The user’s mental model becomes the **dominant framework** in the zone.

- enemy actuation is suppressed or destabilized
- lower-level Actuants may lose control entirely

---

## Domain Properties

### **Bounded Space**

The domain creates a defined area (sphere, field, or structure).  
Range depends on:

- knowledge density
- mental strength
- stability

---

### **Cognitive Imprint**

Each domain reflects the user’s mind:

- a swordsman → battlefield-like space
- a scientist → structured, geometric field
- an unstable mind → distorted, chaotic environment

---

### **Principle Integration**

All Omnipotence Principles operate at peak:

- Aegis becomes near-impenetrable
- Amplification reaches maximum output
- Flow becomes perfectly optimized

---

## Limitations

- **Extreme mental cost**
- **Time-limited activation**
- **Risk of cognitive collapse** if overloaded
- **Stronger domains override weaker ones**

---

## Summary

A Cognitive Domain is not just power—  
it is the moment where:

**“The world temporarily obeys the user’s understanding of it.”**
