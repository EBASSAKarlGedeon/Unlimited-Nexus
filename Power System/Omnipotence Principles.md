# Operational Capacity — Principles of Omnipotence

While **Omniscience defines what is possible**, **Omnipotence defines how it is executed**.
All CAS abilities are the result of mastering **Operational Principles**, the fundamental ways mental strength interacts with reality.

---
## Core Principles

### 1. **Aegis Principle (Envelop / Shroud)**

The user coats their body in mental strength, forming a defensive layer.

* Protects against physical attacks and low-level emotional interference
* Weak against attacks reinforced with higher mental strength

---
### 2. **Nullity Principle (Suppress / Null)**

The reduction or erasure of one’s detectable presence.

* Hides cognitive, sensory, and energetic signatures
* Advanced users can become completely undetectable

---
### 3. **Amplification Principle (Refine / Enhance)**

The explosive outward release of mental strength.

* Increases attack power and intimidation
* High-level use can dominate weaker psyches, inducing fear or unconsciousness

---
### 4. **Imprint Principle (Enfold)**
Mental strength is extended onto external objects.

* Reinforces weapons, tools, or surroundings
* Allows indirect actuation through coated objects

---
## Advanced Principles

### 5. **Fortress Principle (Fortify)**

A fusion of Aegis and Amplification.

* Greatly enhances defense and physical attributes
* Multiplies overall durability and resilience

---
### 6. **Convergence Principle (Focus)**
Concentrates mental strength into a single point.

* Massively boosts power in one area
* Leaves the rest of the body vulnerable

---
### 7. **Dynamic Flow Principle (Flow)**
Adaptive redistribution of mental strength across the body.

* Balances offense and defense dynamically
* Allows partial focus without full vulnerability

---
## Summary
These principles define how Omnipotence is **applied**, not what it creates.
Mastery lies in:
* combining principles
* adjusting output in real time
* and maintaining cognitive stability under pressure
**True CAS abilities are born when perfect Omniscience meets flawless execution of these principles.**
