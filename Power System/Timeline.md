2100–2240: CAS civilization expands while the Dark Wall continues advancing.
2250: Start of the story. By 2250, Earth is a patchwork of fortified cities, wastelands, and contested zones. The **Dark Wall** still advances slowly from Antarctica, leaving vast swaths of ruined terrain behind, infested with evolved Neurovores. Most of the surface outside major hubs is uninhabitable, patrolled by NSF forces and Void Hunters. Human civilization has shifted to **highly centralized, CAS-dependent societies**. Cities float or are walled with cognitive energy barriers; advanced Artifacts are embedded in everyday life, from transport to defense. Education focuses on actuation and neural training, and the **Inter-Noetic Network** serves as a global cognitive backbone.
Survivors rely on **cooperative networks of Actuants**, while rogue Actuants and unregulated Hollows create constant threats. Culture has evolved around survival, innovation, and honor, with Void Hunters celebrated as icons. Humanity has adapted, but the **constant pressure of Neurovores** keeps society on the brink of collapse.
