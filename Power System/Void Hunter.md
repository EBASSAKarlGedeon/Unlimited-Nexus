# Void Hunter Title

The title **Void Hunter** is one of the highest civilian and scientific honors in the CAS era. It is not a military rank, but a **global recognition granted to exceptional individuals whose actions significantly advanced humanity’s survival against Neurovores**.

The title is awarded by the International Cognitive Security Council (ICSC) and carries immense prestige, influence, and resources.

## Conditions for the Title

An individual may be granted the title of Void Hunter through two recognized achievements.

### 1. Defensive Feats

The title can be awarded to individuals who have performed extraordinary acts in protecting human populations from high-level Neurovore threats.

This includes:

- **suppressing**
    
- **repelling**
    
- or **surviving encounters with Class-5 Neurovores**, known as **Hollows**
    

Hollows are among the most dangerous Neurovores known. They possess extremely advanced cognitive absorption and actuation capabilities, often surpassing trained CAS users.

Defeating or forcing one to retreat is considered an achievement of historic scale.

### 2. Revolutionary Innovation

The title can also be granted to individuals whose inventions dramatically strengthen humanity’s ability to combat Neurovores.

Examples include:

- creation of **CAS amplification technology**
    
- development of **neural defense systems**
    
- breakthroughs in **cognitive stabilization methods**
    
- new **detection or containment technologies**
    

Many Void Hunters are therefore **scientists, engineers, and researchers**, not only combat specialists.

## Privileges of a Void Hunter

Void Hunters receive privileges rarely granted to ordinary citizens:

- unrestricted access to advanced CAS research facilities
    
- priority protection by international security organizations
    
- direct collaboration with governments and global scientific bodies
    
- authority to lead independent research or defense initiatives
    

Because of these privileges, Void Hunters are often considered **symbols of humanity’s resistance**.

## Social Status

Although the title is extremely rare, Void Hunters are widely known to the public. Media often portray them as a combination of **scientific pioneers and defenders of civilization**.

However, the title carries an unspoken truth:

**Most Void Hunters earned their recognition in moments where entire cities were on the brink of collapse.**
