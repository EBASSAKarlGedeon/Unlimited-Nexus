## Known Figures and Entities

Records of major individuals and Neurovores are intentionally brief. Many details remain classified or unknown, and most information is revealed gradually through investigation and historical reconstruction.

---
### Aaron Jackson

**Title:** First Void Hunter  
**Alias:** _Siegfried_  
**Type:** Actuant

Aaron Jackson is recognized as the **first individual ever granted the title of Void Hunter**. His title, _Siegfried_, was given after the historic defeat of the Hollow **Kamish**, an enormous dragon-type Neurovore that had devastated several regions.

Jackson’s actuation capabilities focused on extreme thermal and kinetic manipulation, allowing him to engage large-scale threats. His battle with Kamish is considered the first confirmed instance of a human successfully eliminating a **Class-5 Hollow**.

The event marked a turning point in the early CAS era, proving that Hollows could be killed.

---

### Kamish

**Classification:** Hollow Neurovore (N5)  
**Type:** Dragon Morph  
**Height:** Approximately 80 meters

Kamish was one of the earliest and most destructive Hollows recorded. Its form resembled a massive red dragon capable of generating intense flame-based phenomena.

The creature’s morphology is believed to have originated from the **cognitive imprint of victims obsessed with dragons and mythological creatures**. Over time, these patterns stabilized into a giant reptilian structure with high thermal actuation.

Kamish’s defeat by Aaron Jackson ended a catastrophic rampage and remains one of the most studied Hollow incidents in CAS history.
